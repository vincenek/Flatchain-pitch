Core Features

FlatChain's innovative approach to fiat payments combines AI validation, real-time compliance, and tokenless design

AI-Validated Transactions

Replace miners with AI agents for faster confirmations, reduced energy usage, and enhanced fraud detection.

Fiat-Based Ledger

Focus on real-world currencies (USD, EUR, GBP) with cross-border, tax-compliant payments.

AI Processing Layer

Advanced pattern recognition, predictive fraud analytics, and payment optimization.

FlatNodes

Permissioned AI nodes hosted by governments and financial institutions for secure validation.

Real-Time Compliance

Automatic checks against global tax and AML laws for every transaction.

Bank & API Integration

Seamless connection to banking systems for instant fiat-to-fiat transfers.

Transparent Privacy

Public ledger with privacy-preserving encryption and adjustable visibility levels.

Tokenless Design

No native token - transaction fees paid in fiat to reduce speculation.

Smart Agreement Layer

AI-readable logic for tax withholding, regulatory reporting, and payment conditions.

Cross-System Sync

Embed into ERP, accounting, and government systems for real-time ledger syncing.
